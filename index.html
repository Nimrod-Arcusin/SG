Secured General is an international security services company specializing in both public and private sectors. SG provides governments and corporate leaders around the world with tailored security solutions, using military technology and training methodologies. The companies expertise is based on the founders’ long-term experience in the Israeli military and first line security agencies.

About

Avishay Assayag and Tal Sobel met whilst serving in the Israeli military. Both combatants and officers at one of Israel Defense Forces’ Special Forces unit called “Maglan”. This unit specializes in using the most advanced technology and weaponry behind enemy lines. Their experience with “Maglan” have given them the knowledge and skill sets to launch Secure General with confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide security as well as technological solutions for Governments, private customers, and business entities on a global level. The firm consists of a team of experts utilizing their personal experience in the field to prepare, anticipate, and avoid any potential threat. With the responsibility of providing the best services to each community and country we are operating, Secured General operates under international ethical standards to provide the most advanced regulatory and secured services.
