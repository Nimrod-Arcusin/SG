Secured General is an international security services company working with the public and private sector. The company is providing customized security methods, tailored technology, military, and security courses to VIP corporate leaders and governments around the world. Its expertise is based on the founders’ long-term experience from the Israeli military and first line security agencies.

About the founders

Avishay Assayag and Tal Sobel have known each other from their military service. Both combatants and officers at one of Israel Defense Forces’ Special Forces units called “Maglan”. This unit’s specialization is operating using the most advanced technology and weaponry behind the enemy line and its territory. Their experience at this unit has given them each skill sets for their latter experience to eventually founding this firm.

Avishay Assayag

Multiple years of experience at the “Shin Bet” (the Israel Security Agency, Israel’s internal security service) servicing Israel in different roles

  • Personal bodyguard of Israel’s prime minister, president and the security minister
  • Managing various missions related to homeland security both in Israel and abroad
  • Managing a technology team, responsible for using the most advanced technological tools and systems
  • Deputy director of security trainings

Tal Sobel

Started his career by working for several years at the “Israeli Witness Protection Authority”, one of many units belonging to Israel’s internal security service.

Later one being a logistics and purchasing operations manager at one of Israeli’s leading pharmaceutical and natural health products companies.

  • Direct contact with the company’s executives and regulatory agencies
  • Managing the distribution and logistics team
  • Working with several security firms to ensure local security of the company’s factory, warehouse and local shops

Our Mission

Our goal and mission are to provide on a global level security and technology solutions to governments, private customers and business entities. With the aim to avoid, prepare, anticipate and respond to any potential threat. The firm is combining a team of experts utilizing their personal experience in the field as well as the most advanced technology. Secured General is operating amongst international ethical standards, the most advanced regulatory and secured service with the responsibility of providing the best services to each community and country we are operating.
